Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Color Rush ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Color Rush does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or account credentials directly.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Interaction data with ads
  • Crash and performance data

This information is used for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Rewarded ads (e.g. continue gameplay)
  • Improving ad relevance
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

For more information on how Google uses data collected from apps, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Advertising

Color Rush displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads and rewarded ads.

Rewarded ads are optional and are used to provide in-game benefits (such as continuing a run).

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

4. Data Security

The developer does not store or process personal data directly. All data handling is managed by third-party services in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

Color Rush is not directed at children under the age of 13.

The developer does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

6. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Requests regarding user data should be addressed to Google via their privacy controls.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
